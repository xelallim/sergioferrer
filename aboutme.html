About me

Science Communication & Journalism

Based in Valencia, Spain & London, United Kingdom

I have been working in the field of Science Journalism and Science Communication in Spain for the last 10 years. Having published in numerous local and national publications publications in Spain, I am also currently the Science Editor for The Conversation España, a founding member of SMC and a regular contributor in El Diario and Agencia Sinc. I have spoken at and moderated panels such as The Women in Science Panel and This Other Panel and am the receipiant of This Prize and This Other Prize, as well as teaching on science communication courses at various institutions around Spain.
